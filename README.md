# AI-in-Risk-Assessment
A container for research, tools, and analysis collected for the paper Justice by Design: A Survey of AI-enabled Risk Assessment in Criminal Justice

## Authors
Gentry Atkinson, PhD
St. Edwards University.

Katlyn Casagrande, PhD
Utah State University

## Research Questions
- Is machine learning being used as a tool for risk assessment in the United States?
- What specific AI & ML technologies are being applied for risk assessment?
- Does algorithmic risk assessment contribute to bias or issues of fairness in sentencing, bail, or parole?
- What, if any, is the evidence and apparent source of bias in algorithmic risk assessment?
